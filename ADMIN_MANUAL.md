# MyCatholic Admin Manual

Welcome to the MyCatholic Admin Dashboard. This guide will help you manage users, content, and partners.

## 1. How to Verify Users (Umat)
**Menu:** `Verifikasi` (Sidebar)

1.  **View List**: You will see a list of users with "Menunggu" (Pending) status.
2.  **Filter**: You can use the search bar to find a specific name or email.
3.  **Review**: Click the **Detail** button on a user row.
4.  **Action**:
    -   A modal will appear showing the user's details and uploaded document (e.g., Baptism Certificate).
    -   Click **Buka Full Size** to view the document clearly.
    -   **Terima & Verifikasi**: Click to approve. The user will get a green "Verified" badge.
    -   **Tolak**: Click to reject if the document is invalid.

## 2. However to Manage CMS (Content)
**Menu:** `CMS` (Sidebar)

### Managing Articles (Berita)
1.  Click the **Articles / Berita** tab.
2.  **Create**: Click **+ Tulis Berita**.
    -   Upload a cover image.
    -   Enter Title and Content.
    -   Click **Simpan**.
3.  **publish**: Toggle the switch in the "Published" column to show/hide the article in the app.
4.  **Edit/Delete**: Use the icons in the Action column.

### Managing Announcements (Pengumuman)
1.  Click the **Pengumuman** tab.
2.  **Create**: Click **+ Buat Pengumuman**.
    -   Select Target Audience:
        -   **Nasional**: Visible to everyone.
        -   **Keuskupan**: Select a specific Diocese.
        -   **Paroki**: Select a specific Parish (Church).
    -   Enter Title and Message.
3.  **Active Status**: Toggle the switch to enable or disable the announcement.

## 3. How to Verify Partners (Mitra Pastoral)
**Menu:** `Mitra Pastoral` (Sidebar)

Use this menu to verify specialized roles: **Pastor**, **Suster**, **Frater**, **Bruder**.

1.  **View List**: You will see candidates who applied for pastoral roles.
2.  **Check Role**: The list shows the applied role and their Order (Ordo/Kongregasi).
3.  **Review**: Click **Review**.
4.  **Verify Document**: Check the uploaded **Surat Celebret** or **Surat Tugas**.
5.  **Approve**: Click **Terima & Verifikasi**. This grants them the specialized role in the app (e.g., allowing them to lead 'Tele-Ministry' sessions).

## 4. Troubleshooting

### Stats showing "0"
If the Dashboard numbers show "0" but you know there is data:
1.  **Refresh the Page**: Sometimes the connection needs a reset.
2.  **Check Internet**: Ensure you are connected to the internet.
3.  **Wait**: Real-time stats may take 1-2 seconds to load.

### Cannot Upload Images
-   Ensure the image file is not too large (max 5MB recommended).
-   If "Permission Denied", ask the IT Admin to check the Storage Bucket Policies (`article_images`).
